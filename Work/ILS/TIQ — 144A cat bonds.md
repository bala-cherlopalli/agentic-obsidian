# TIQ — 144A cat bonds

Short reference for **ILS / cat bond** work in TIQ. Moody’s often deals with **144A**-structured risk (private placement to QIBs).

- **Rule 144A** — U.S. rule for **private placements** of securities (including cat bonds) to **qualified institutional buyers (QIBs)** without full SEC registration; common for institutional ILS in the U.S.
- **Cat bonds** — **catastrophe bonds**: insurance-linked securities that transfer natural-catastrophe risk from (re)insurers to capital-market investors; principal at risk if triggers hit, coupon in return.
- **144A cat bonds** — Cat bond issues offered under Rule 144A. “144A risk” in workshop shorthand often means the **risk transfer** is documented and placed in that **144A private-placement** layer.
